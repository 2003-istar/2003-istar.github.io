Featured Project

Study Buddy

GitHub

An application designed to help students tackle dense academic readings through structured, manageable chunks. Study Buddy applies the Pomodoro method to reading, allowing users to upload PDFs, break them into customizable sections, and work through them with timed intervals and breaks. The app includes annotation and highlighting features, text-to-speech functionality, and an AI-powered chatbot for discussing the material.

I built Study Buddy to address my own struggles with comprehending dense sociology readings and to fill a gap I identified in accessibility resources at my school's disability center. It was my first full-stack project, giving me hands-on experience with architectural decisions and technology research from the ground up.

Key Features:

  • PDF chunking with customizable section timing and break intervals
  • In-document annotation, highlighting, and note-saving
  • Text-to-speech for accessibility
  • RAG-based AI chatbot for discussing readings
  • Downloadable annotated PDFs
  • State machine architecture managing 6 distinct screens
